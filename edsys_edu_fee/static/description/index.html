Education Fee Master

Maintaining transparency between stakeholders regarding the finances becomes easier with all the fee structure being configured on a common platform.
Automated systems improve accuracy of financial dealings as opposed to manual procedures that are error prone and cause constant delays.

In addition, an organized account of fees on a secure digital platform, improves regulatory compliance.

Features

  • Configure with account payment
  • Calculate monthly fees
  • Set up discounts
  • Organize fee structure
  • update student fee
  • generate fee history
  • update outstanding and paid fee details
  • resend payfort link
  • create parent and student payment report

This module supports monthly fee calculation for all grades. On selecting the specific month, the details of fees of the selected grade are displayed.

Fee Structure - This section holds the account of fee structure for all the grades. Both registration and annual fees for specific year can be viewed here.

Fee Discount - Students recieve concessions for school amenities like books, uniforms, tuition fees based on certain criteria. This module keeps the details of
discounted fees on track.

TC Fee structure - This section defines the details of transfer certificate charges once the student applies of a TC.
